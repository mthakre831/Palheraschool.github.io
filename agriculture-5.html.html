ABOUT AGRICULTURE

Wondering what to do after 12th in agriculture? Are you looking for the best Agricultural courses after 12th in Commerce, Science and Arts? Agricultural Science is surely emerging as a popular field of study not only for students completing their 12th with BiPC subjects but also for those aiming to study Agribusiness, Agricultural Engineering, etc. From Bachelor of Science to Bachelor of Technology, the field has a diverse range of courses on offer. Read this blog to know about the best agricultural courses after 12th Science, Arts, and Commerce and pick the right course as per your interests!



With technology taking over almost every sector including Agriculture, it has now become the topmost contributor to the Indian economy. Over the past few years, many major technological developments and techniques have been incorporated in crop production thus leading led to the enhancement of quantity as well as the quality of the crops produced. This has led to a dire need of skilled individuals who can contribute to maintaining the same at each and every step of the process. The agriculture sector generates job opportunities for people from all walks of life. Ranging from farmers and field workers to technicians and scientists, the domain is full of promising career opportunities.



When it comes to choosing the right course to study Agriculture after 12th, students are presented with a wide range of options. There are many short-term agriculture courses after 12th such as diplomas and certifications to full-fledged degree courses like Bachelor of Science and Bachelor of Technology. Also, there are tons of specializations that students can avail such as Agronomy, and Fisheries, under the above-mentioned degrees.
